Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Never Roll a 1 is a simple browser-based game. We collect minimal information:

  • Game statistics stored locally in your browser (scores, streaks)
  • Anonymous analytics data (page views, game sessions)

Cookies

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Save your game progress and statistics
  • Deliver advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Analyze site traffic and usage

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site. Data is collected anonymously.
  • Google Firebase: To provide authentication and leaderboard functionality. If you sign in, we store your display name, user ID, and game scores in Cloud Firestore.

Data Storage

All game data (scores, streaks, attempts) is stored locally in your browser. We do not collect or store this information on our servers. Leaderboard data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore and includes only your chosen display name, anonymous user ID, win streak, and attempt count.

Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the contact page.
