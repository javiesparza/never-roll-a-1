Contact Us

Have questions, feedback, or found a bug? We'd love to hear from you! We typically respond within 48 hours on weekdays. Before reaching out, you may find your answer in our FAQ.

Email

contact@neverrollone.com

For general inquiries, partnership opportunities, or press requests. We read every message and aim to reply within two business days.

Report a Bug

Found something broken? Let us know and we'll fix it ASAP. When reporting a bug, please include your browser name and version, your device type (desktop, phone, tablet), and a description of what happened versus what you expected. Screenshots are always helpful. Bug reports are prioritized and typically addressed within 24 hours.

Feature Requests

Have ideas for new features or game modes? We're all ears! Some of the game's best features, including the win streak tracker and the daily leaderboard, started as player suggestions. When submitting a feature request, tell us what you would like to see and why it would make the game better. We cannot implement every idea, but we carefully evaluate each suggestion against our goal of keeping the game simple, fun, and educational.

Legal & Privacy

For questions about your data, privacy rights, or our terms, please see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. If you have a specific legal inquiry, reach out via email and include "Legal" in the subject line.
